About Me

I'm a passionate sophomore at Lotus School for Excellence with a deep commitment to education, leadership, and community empowerment. As the Vice President of both the Muslim Student Association and Black Student Association, I've dedicated myself to creating inclusive spaces where every voice is heard and valued.

"Every student is a bright mind - they just need support to shine. I'm here to help you discover your potential."

— Salahdiin Ahmed Adan, Founder of Bright Minds Tutoring

My journey as an educator began with a simple belief: every student has incredible potential. This conviction led me to found Bright Minds Tutoring, where I provide free, personalized academic support to students who need it most.

As a GripTape Challenger, I continue to explore my passions in law, public speaking, and healthcare, always seeking ways to make a positive impact in my community. I believe that leadership isn't about titles—it's about service, empathy, and the courage to create change.
